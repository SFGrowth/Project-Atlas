## Part 5 — Validation Rules Enforcement

### Mandatory Execution Protocol

Every S109-001 signal must be executed without discretion. The following rules are non-negotiable during the evaluation period:

**Signal Execution**
- Every signal generated by S109-001 must be taken — no skipping, no cherry-picking
- No discretionary trades outside S109-001 signals
- No parameter changes — the hypothesis is frozen exactly as defined in Sprint 109
- 1 MNQ contract per signal — no scaling in or out
- $450 risk per trade — no changes

**Daily Protection Protocol**
The Apex 50K trailing drawdown limit is $2,000. With $450/trade risk:

| Consecutive Losses | DD Used | Remaining Buffer | Action |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | $450 (22.5%) | $1,550 | Continue normally |
| 2 | $900 (45.0%) | $1,100 | Increase vigilance, confirm all 3 filters |
| 3 | $1,350 (67.5%) | $650 | Mandatory review before next trade |
| 4 | $1,800 (90.0%) | $200 | **STOP — investigate before continuing** |

**Divergence Classification**
After every trade, record the actual execution vs Atlas signal:

| Flag | Condition | Action |
|---|---|---|
| NONE | Exact match | Log and continue |
| EXPECTED_SLIPPAGE | Exit slippage ≤ 1.25 pts | Log and continue |
| ELEVATED_SLIPPAGE | Exit slippage > 1.25 pts | Investigate broker/time-of-day |
| OUTCOME_DIVERGENCE | Win/loss disagrees with Atlas | Immediate investigation |
| EXECUTION_ERROR | Wrong direction, wrong size | Stop and review |

**Promotion Gate (Walk-Forward → Paper Trading)**
- Minimum 20 completed trades
- Win Rate ≥ 65%
- Profit Factor ≥ 2.0
- No critical divergence flags (OUTCOME_DIVERGENCE or EXECUTION_ERROR)
- Outcome match rate ≥ 90%
