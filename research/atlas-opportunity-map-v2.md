# Atlas Opportunity Map v2.0
**Date:** July 2026  
**Status:** Re-ranked post-RMCE Validation

## The Post-RMCE Reality

Sprint 046 proved that data-mining historical events (RMCE) produces hypotheses with high survivorship bias and low forward-expectancy. Atlas must return to structural, theory-driven hypothesis generation.

The execution matrix (Models A1, A2, A3) is complete. The portfolio risk layer (ARI v2.0) is complete. The outstanding failure point is the Prop Firm Execution Layer (ATS v1.0 failed MC simulations due to concurrent risk stacking).

The Opportunity Map is now re-ranked to prioritise the final engineering steps required to achieve the project's primary mission: passing prop firm evaluations.

## Top 5 Research Priorities (Ranked by Expected Value)

### 1. ARI v3.0: Prop Firm Engineering (Sprint 044 Implementation)
* **Objective:** Implement the validated Sprint 044 findings (Policy C: Priority Queue) into the permanent ARI architecture, combined with milestone-based risk compounding.
* **Expected Value:** Critical path. This is the exact mechanism required to convert ATS v1.0 from EXPERIMENTAL to PROMOTED.
* **Status:** Ready for immediate engineering.

### 2. Model B1 Discovery: Mean Reversion
* **Objective:** Discover a negative-correlation model to complement the trend-following A-series models.
* **Expected Value:** High. A validated mean-reversion model would fundamentally alter the portfolio's drawdown profile, reducing the burden on ARI.
* **Status:** Hypothesis generation required. Focus on exhaustion signatures (V-shape recoveries) in low-ADX regimes.

### 3. The AM Session Liquidity Sweep
* **Objective:** The RMCE proved the AM session contains massive volatility but breakout models fail. The logical inverse is a liquidity sweep model (fade the initial AM breakout).
* **Expected Value:** High. Exploits the exact false-breakout frequency that destroyed H-RMCE-01.
* **Status:** Hypothesis generation required.

### 4. Dynamic Target Scaling
* **Objective:** Replace fixed 2.0R targets with dynamic targets based on regime strength.
* **Expected Value:** Moderate. Could significantly improve the Profit Factor of Model A1 and A3 during exceptional trend days.
* **Status:** Ready for testing.

### 5. Multi-Timeframe Regime Alignment
* **Objective:** Require the 15-minute or 60-minute ADX to align with the 5-minute ADX before permitting Model A2/A3 entries.
* **Expected Value:** Moderate. A classic structural filter that has not yet been rigorously tested in the Atlas framework.
* **Status:** Ready for testing.
